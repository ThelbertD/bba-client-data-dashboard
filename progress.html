Seven new features for Jase's demo

The features named in the kickoff call as critical for the Phase 2 + Phase 3 demo. All live in the dashboard right now.

★
Churn rate · headline KPI
Big top-row card. Lifetime cancelled ÷ total ever. Click-through to the cancelled client list.
★
Retention rate · headline KPI
The mirror of churn. Active ÷ (active + cancelled). Click-through to the still-active book.
★
Renewal rate · headline KPI
Clients on extra weeks ÷ clients past onboarding. Surfaces the upsell base.
★
Off-boarding split · headline KPI
Single card showing total off-boarded plus a visual paying-vs-challenge bar split.
★
Support coach leaderboard
Every coach ranked by retention with active / cancelled / contract value side by side.
★
Whole-dashboard coach filter
Pick a coach from the top selector and every KPI, chart, calendar, and table narrows to their book.
★
Chat answers match the KPI cards
Ask "what's our churn rate?" in the chat — the number returned matches the headline card exactly, by reading from the same source. ✓ Demo acceptance criterion met.
